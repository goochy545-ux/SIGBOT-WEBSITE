Data Processing Addendum (DPA)

Effective Date: February 2026

1. Scope

This Data Processing Addendum ("DPA") applies to the processing of Personal Data by Sigbot on behalf of the Customer as part of the Service.

2. Definitions

Terms such as "Controller", "Processor", "Data Subject", and "Personal Data" shall have the meanings given in the applicable Data Protection Laws (GDPR, UK GDPR, etc.).

3. Roles

The Customer is the Controller and Sigbot is the Processor.

4. Data Processing

Sigbot will process Personal Data only on documented instructions from the Customer, including with regard to transfers of personal data to a third country.

5. Sub-processors

The Customer grants general authorization to Sigbot to engage sub-processors (e.g., Google Cloud, Firebase, Stripe). Sigbot shall ensure sub-processors are bound by data protection obligations compatible with this DPA.

6. Security

Sigbot implements appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure a level of security appropriate to the risk.

7. Download

To execute this DPA, please download the signed PDF version, countersign it, and return it to legal@sigbot.co.
