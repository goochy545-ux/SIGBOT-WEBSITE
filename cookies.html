Cookie Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website function properly and provide analytics.

2. How we use cookies

Sigbot uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function, such as managing your login session.
  • Analytics Cookies: To understand how visitors use our site (e.g., Google Analytics). These help us improve the user experience.

3. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling essential cookies may impact the functionality of the Sigbot website.
