Common questions

Does Sigbot upload my data?
Contacts are synced to a secure Firebase cloud database for cross-device access. No data is shared with third parties.
What does it capture?
Signature fields like name, title, company, phone, email.
Can I export contacts?
Yes — CSV and Excel export for your tools.
Does it deduplicate?
Yes — it keeps one clean record per contact.
How do updates work?
The web app updates automatically — just refresh your browser to get the latest version.
What email providers are supported?
Outlook, any IMAP-compatible provider (including Gmail via an app password), or email forwarding from any mailbox.
Is there a mobile app?
Yes — iOS and Android. Search contacts, view maps, scan business cards, and share your profile via QR code.

Troubleshooting

Can’t log in
Try resetting your password or clearing your browser cache.
Inbox not connecting
Check that you’ve granted the necessary permissions when connecting your account.
Contacts not appearing
Allow a few minutes for the initial sync to complete after connecting an inbox.
