Common questions

Do I need a credit card to start?
No. The Starter plan is free forever with no card required. Pro and Team come with a 30-day money-back guarantee — if you're not satisfied, email us within 30 days for a full refund.
What happens when I hit the 50-contact limit?
Sigbot stops adding new contacts. Your existing 50 are safe. Upgrade to Pro to remove the limit instantly.
Can I switch plans at any time?
Yes. Upgrade or downgrade whenever you like. If you downgrade, your data is preserved — contacts above the new limit are hidden until you upgrade again.
Is there a discount for annual billing?
Yes — toggle to annual above to save 20% on Pro ($192/yr) and Team ($1,920/yr).
How does Team pricing work beyond 10 users?
The Team plan includes up to 10 users. Additional seats are $6/user/month. Contact us for larger deployments.
