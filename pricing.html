Common questions

Do I need a credit card to start?
No. The Starter plan is free forever with no card required. Pro and Team come with a 30-day money-back guarantee — if you're not satisfied, email us within 30 days for a full refund.
What happens when I hit the Starter scan limit?
Starter scans up to 300 emails in total (50 per day). Once you reach the limit, Sigbot stops scanning new emails — your existing contacts are safe. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited scanning at up to 2,000 emails/day, plus a full backfill of your inbox history.
Can I switch plans at any time?
Yes. Upgrade or downgrade whenever you like. If you downgrade, your data is preserved — contacts above the new limit are hidden until you upgrade again.
Is there a discount for yearly billing?
Yes — toggle to yearly above to save 20% on Pro ($192/yr) and Team ($1,920/yr).
How does Team pricing work?
One flat price covers up to 10 users sharing a single contact database — no per-seat charges. Need more than 10 users? Talk to us about an Enterprise plan.
